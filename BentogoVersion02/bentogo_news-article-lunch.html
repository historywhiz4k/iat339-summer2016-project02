What's in your lunch?.

Say good-bye to sandwiches and say hello to variety
Vancouverites have variety of optioins when it comes to their lunch menu

They say you are what you eat and that couldn't be more true to the people of Vancouver.

With Vancouver's multi-cultural selection of different restaurants offering foods from around the world, one's lunch selection a hard one to make.

While fast food is the go-to choice for most hard-working people of Vancouver, more and more are starting to dine in at the various Cafe's and restaurants for a more healthy and delectable lunch. With many establishments offering take-out lunch menus, it caters to workers who want to take time with their lunch and to those who needs to go back to work as soon as possible with their food.
