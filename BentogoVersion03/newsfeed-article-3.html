BENTOGO @ Vancouver Sushi Festival 2016

The annual sushi festival was held at downtown Vancouver. We explored to find the tastiest Sushi that Vancouver has to offer.

Date: 06/20/2016 | Written by: SUSHIMEISTER

Vancouver is the home to many sushi restaurants with their own unique take on the iconinc Japanese food. The annual festival is where different sushi chefs come to show off their skills and enjoy tasty sushi with the community. BENTOGO attended this year's festival to see what kind of sushi we can find and treat ourselves.

To celebrate the Sushi Festival, BENTOGO will be offering sushi BENTOGO during the duration of the festival! Pick up a Sushi BENTOGO today!
