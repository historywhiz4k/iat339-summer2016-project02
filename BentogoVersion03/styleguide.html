Paragraphs

Sample Code:

<p>When it comes to food in Japan, many people would think of sushi, ramen or the fantastic variety of alcoholic beverages. It may come as a shock, but like many westerners, Japanese people enjoy fried foods and is part of their main diet in a common household. If we have fried chicken in the west, then in the east, they have Karaage; a different take on one of the most iconic comfort foods ever.</p>

Rendered Element:

When it comes to food in Japan, many people would think of sushi, ramen or the fantastic variety of alcoholic beverages. It may come as a shock, but like many westerners, Japanese people enjoy fried foods and is part of their main diet in a common household. If we have fried chicken in the west, then in the east, they have Karaage; a different take on one of the most iconic comfort foods ever.
